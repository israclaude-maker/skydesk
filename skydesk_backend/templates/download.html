About

What is SkyDesk?

SkyDesk is a lightweight remote desktop application that lets you connect to and control another computer over the internet, using nothing more than a unique Remote ID.

Whether you're providing tech support to a family member, accessing your work PC from home, or helping a colleague troubleshoot an issue, SkyDesk gives you a live view of the remote screen along with full mouse and keyboard control, all through an encrypted connection.

There's nothing to configure on your router or network. Install the app, sign in, share your ID with the person on the other end, and you're connected within seconds.

SkyDesk was built to strip away the complexity found in many remote access tools, focusing instead on a fast, dependable connection that just works when you need it most.

Getting Started

How to use it

Install SkyDesk

Download and run the installer on your Windows PC. Setup takes less than a minute.

Sign in

Log in with your account to receive your unique Remote ID, generated automatically.

Share your ID

Give your Remote ID to whoever needs access, or enter theirs to connect to them.

Connect instantly

Accept the request on either end and the session begins immediately, fully encrypted.

Security

Built with privacy in mind

Every SkyDesk session runs over an encrypted connection, so what happens on the remote screen and every keystroke or mouse movement sent during control stays between the two connected devices.

Sessions only begin after an explicit request is accepted on the receiving end, meaning no one can access your computer without your direct approval at the moment of connection.
